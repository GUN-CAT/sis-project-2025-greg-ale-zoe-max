Our monkeys:

Finger Monkeys:

The finger monkey, or pygmy marmoset (Callithrix pygmaea), is the world’s smallest monkey, native to the rainforests of South America. At just 12–15 cm long (plus tail) and weighing 100–140 grams, these tiny primates are incredibly agile and social. They thrive in groups, communicate with high-pitched calls, and love climbing. Their playful nature, small size, and low-maintenance diet (tree sap, insects, and fruit) make them a unique and entertaining companion—perfect for anyone looking for an exotic, interactive pet experience!

Squirrel Monkey

Squirrel monkeys are small, energetic primates with bright yellow fur and playful personalities. Native to Central and South America, they love to interact with people and explore their surroundings. Perfect for events, photoshoots, or just adding a fun twist, these curious monkeys will steal the show!

The Macaque:

A macaque monkey is an intelligent, social, and highly active primate known for its playful nature and expressive face. These curious creatures are quick learners and can form strong bonds with their owners. Renting a macaque as a pet offers a unique experience of interacting with a highly engaging and entertaining animal. However, they require specialized care, attention, and enrichment to stay happy and healthy. Perfect for those seeking a temporary, lively companion with a mischievous charm!
